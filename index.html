Welcome to NCPOR (erstwhile NCAOR)

National Centre for Polar and Ocean Research (NCPOR) is India's premier R&D institution responsible for the country's research activities in the polar and Southern Ocean realms.

Polar Science & Cryosphere

The Polar Science & Cryosphere studies at NCPOR are aimed at a holistic understanding of the polar regions.

Geoscience

The Geoscientific Programmes at NCPOR seek to carry out a systematic geoscientific survey of the Ocean realm around India.
